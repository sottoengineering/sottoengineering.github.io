At SOTTO ENGINEERING, we offer expert electrical solutions for maritime, commercial, and industrial clients. From motor and generator repairs to rewinding and maintenance, we provide comprehensive services to keep your operations running smoothly.

Our team of experienced professionals is committed to providing reliable, cost-effective solutions tailored to your unique needs. Trust us to keep your business moving forward.

Best Quality Repair Services

SOTTO ENGINEERING is a family-owned and operated business that has been providing high-quality electrical repair services for over 30 years. Our founder, Mr. Francisco “Cocoy” O. Sotto, started the business with his hands-on repair skills and his dedication to providing exceptional customer service. Today, Sotto Engineering continues to uphold those values, providing a wide range of electrical repair services, including electric motor and generator repairs.

At Sotto Engineering, we believe that customer satisfaction and trust are essential to our success. That's why we strive to exceed our clients' expectations by providing prompt, reliable, and cost-effective repair solutions. Our team of experienced technicians, led by an engineering expert, is committed to delivering quality workmanship and exceptional service, ensuring that our clients' operations run smoothly and efficiently.

As a family-owned business, we take pride in our reputation as a trusted provider of electrical repair services in our local community. We are committed to continuing our legacy of excellence, providing innovative solutions and exceptional customer service for years to come.
